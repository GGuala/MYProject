wHat I do

I make sculptural art that asks where the spiritual lives in the physical world.

I direct the visual language of brands and campaigns — building universes before assets.

aBout

I've been making things since I was sixteen. What started as a need to understand the world through form became a practice that crosses sculpture, digital art, and visual direction.

My work circles the same question I've been asking my whole life: where does the spiritual live in the physical world? That question drives what I make — and it's the same instinct I bring to every project I work on. Before aesthetics, I think about meaning.

I'm drawn to problems that look disconnected until they suddenly aren't. Robotics, immersive art, engineering — to me it's all the same puzzle. I'm drawn to the ones that look unsolvable until they suddenly aren't.
