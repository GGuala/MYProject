wHat I do

I make sculptural art that asks where the spiritual lives in the physical world.

I direct the visual language of brands and campaigns — building universes before assets.

I work on installations and spatial experiences, including technically complex ones. Robotics, immersive environments, engineering constraints — these are the interesting part.

aBout

My practice spans sculpture, photography, and technology around a single pursuit: finding the pause in a world in a hurry.

I look for flashes in the ordinary — a light, a gesture, a silence that interrupts the noise. That sensibility is the engine of my work and the experience I seek to provoke in those who encounter it.

I operate from two gestures: sculpture that builds and invokes those moments, and photography that captures and constructs them when they appear. In every case, I seek to create the conditions for the viewer to stop — and with that second of pause, perhaps change the world.
