about

Artist, designer, director.

I'm Nicolás Guala. My practice moves between sculpture, photography, painting, and graphic design — not because I can't decide, but because the work demands it.

Today I focus on digital sculpting and 3D printing. I'm drawn to creating experiences where the viewer becomes an active participant. Real art emerges when it's shaped by those who engage with it.

selected work

Works

sculpture · 3d print 2025

Ethos

Divinity through sculptural forms that impose themselves in space. The hand as the maker's tool, the face as a mirror of the other. These forms refuse beginnings or endings — they break, branch, and fold into themselves.

Spirituality as an unconscious force, creating itself in the moment, fragile yet resonant.

installation · 3d print 2024

Cementerio

Each piece a fossil of emotion — grief, nostalgia, tenderness — once intangible, now fixed in fragile metallic forms resting on ash.

Personal and collective: a quiet anatomy of memory that transforms inner landscapes into matter.

participatory installation 2024

Estación N3

Viewers become creators. Around a communal table with tools, tape, and sculptural elements, participants cut, glue, paint, and play — their gestures layering into a shared composition.

Authorship dissolves. Creation becomes collective and alive.

conceptual installation 2023

Mónada

Duality and spiritual transformation. In dialogue with Entidad, it reflects on the contradictions of existence — chaos and stillness, matter and spirit, routine and revelation.

Divinity emerges not in transcendence, but in the lightness hidden within ordinary moments.
